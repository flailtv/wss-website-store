About

While She Sleeps is a metal-core band originating from Sheffield, England.
Created in 2006, the band consists of vocalist Lawrence Taylor, guitarists Sean Long and Mat Welsh, bassist
Arron McKenzie and drummer Adam Savage. Currently, the band is signed to SharpTone Records
in the US, UNFD in Australia and an independent band in the UK.
